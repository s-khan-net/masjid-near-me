Help Basic usage
   When you start this application, it locates you on the map , and gets the nearest masjids , masjid names are listed on the menu. On touching the marker, or on a name from the menu, the details of the masjid are shown. You can navigate to the masjid by touching the button in the description window. (this will open google maps).

   The masjids loaded on the menu, can be verified (the ones present in our database) or unverified (the ones on google maps).
The verifed masjids have data about the Salaah times & more details thst can be viewed when you tap the marker or if you tsp in the name of the masjid in the menu. The details can be edited by users with edit permissions.
Settings & Users
The Search radius, Maslak (Hanafi or Shafi), Salaah times calculation method can be set from the Settings page.
Users can signUp/signIn using their email and password from the Users page. Watch this video to see how you can seamlessly sign up from the app. When a new user is registered, 'default' permissions are assigned. The user can request for 'edit' from the user profile page. The request will be sent to the admin for approval. Watch this video to see how you can request for elevated permissions.
More usage details
   The masjids loaded on the menu, can be verified (the ones present in our database) or unverified (the ones on google maps).
 Registered users with edit permissions, can edit the details by using button, this will make the fields editable. The changes can be saved by touching the button. The changes will be reflected immediately on the map. Watch a demo here:
Verifying masjids can be done choosing the options to verify a masjid from the edit window. When an unverified masjid is selected, the option to mark a masjid as verified is available. After a masjid is verified, the details are reflected immediatly on the map. Watch a demo here:

Privacy Policy for Masjid Near Me

Effective Date: 19th April 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Masjid Near Me ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our Android app.

- Google maps

We use Google Maps services to provide you with information about masjids that are near your location. The masjids are displayed as markers on a map and the data for each masjid is fetched from Google Maps. We store this informatin in addition to extra information about the masjid such as the prayer times.

2. Information We Collect

Location Data: To provide you with information about nearby masjids, we obtain your device's precise location using location services on your device. This data is essential for the core functionality of our app. This data is not sent off the app and is never saved anywhere.

Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use our app, including interactions with the app's features. This helps us improve the app's performance and your user experience.

User registration information: We do not require any user registration or personal information to use the website. But to use features like "Edit a Masjid" or "Verify a Masjid", you will be required to register with your email address. This information is stored in our database and is used to verify the user. We do not share this information with any third parties or use it for any other purpose.

3. How We Use Your Information

Location Data: Your location data is used to identify and display masjids near you. The information about your location is not stored on our servers or shared with third parties.

Usage Data: Usage data (stored as logs) helps us understand app performance and identify areas for improvement. This data is aggregated and anonymized, ensuring it cannot be used to identify you.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share any information from this app with third parties. Niether do we have any cookies or trackers being saved.

5. Data Security

We take data security seriously and have implemented measures to protect your information from unauthorized access. Out API is located at https://api.masjidnear.me ad is SSL secured. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Your Rights

Access and Update: You have the right to access and update your personal information. If you need assistance, please contact us.

Withdrawal of Consent: You can withdraw your consent to the collection and use of your location data at any time by adjusting your device settings. Note that disabling location data will affect the app's core functionality.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will notify you by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
